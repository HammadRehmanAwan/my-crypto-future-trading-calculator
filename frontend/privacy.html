Privacy Notice

Last updated: 5 June 2026

FutureX is a free, educational crypto futures calculator. This notice explains what limited personal data we handle and your rights under the UK GDPR and EU GDPR.

What we collect

  • Email address (optional): Only if you choose to enable Volatility Alerts and enter your email. It is used solely to send you the alert emails you requested.
  • Alert preferences: The coins you watch, your sensitivity setting, and your consent flag.

Where it is stored

Your email and preferences are always stored locally in your own browser (via localStorage). In addition, if you sign in with Google to enable 24/7 alerts, they are stored in our Google Firebase (Firestore) database so our server can monitor your coins while your browser is closed. If you never sign in, nothing is stored on our servers.

How alerts are processed

When you are signed in, a scheduled server job (running roughly every 30 minutes) reads your saved preferences from Firestore, checks live market data for the coins you watch, and — only when a volatility threshold you chose is crossed — sends you an email. The job also stores a small "last sent" timestamp per coin in Firestore so you are not emailed repeatedly for the same event.

Who it is shared with

Your data is handled by two processors acting on our behalf: Google Firebase (storage) and EmailJS (email delivery). No other parties receive your data. We never sell or advertise to you.

Legal basis

We process your email on the basis of your explicit consent, which you give by ticking the consent box before saving. You can withdraw consent at any time.

Your rights

  • Access & portability: Your browser copy is visible in your browser's developer tools; your signed-in copy is the same data held in Firestore.
  • Erasure: Click "Delete my stored email & settings" in the Volatility Alerts card. This clears your browser storage and, when you are signed in, deletes your record from our Firestore database — stopping all server alerts immediately.
  • Withdraw consent: Untick the consent box and save, or delete your data as above. You can also disable alerts with the toggle.

Market data

Price, sentiment and news data are fetched directly from third-party APIs (CoinGecko, alternative.me, CryptoCompare and Hugging Face). Your browser contacts these services directly; we do not log those requests.

Contact

For any privacy question, contact the project maintainer via the GitHub repository.

Not financial advice. FutureX is for education only. Crypto futures trading carries substantial risk of loss.
